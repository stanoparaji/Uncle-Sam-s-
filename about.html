About Us

Uncle Sam's was founded in the year 2024 in a small cottage in the heart of New York City due to the love of gourmet cuisines. It all started as a family business by Samuel Lockhurst. Because of his flair for cooking traditionally inspired dishes, he started a small restaurant where hungry customers would line up to have a delicious bite of his menu.

Uncle Sam's is renowned for making mouth-watering local and intercontinental cuisines that delivers a 5-star dining experience.

We have a hospitable team that caters to your everyday needs ranging from local to intercontinental cuisines.
