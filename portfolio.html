My Reputation As An Executive Chef

I have a reputation for being attentive to detail when preparing gourmet cuisines that fits the tastebuds of our esteemed customers ranging from local to intercontinental guests. My restaurant has been awarded a 5-star Medal of Honour and also nominated for the James Beard Awards for being the best in New York City and its environs in the Best Restaurant Global Awards.
And more importantly, Uncle Sam's is known for being timely in delivering customer orders as we prioritize our customers' need.

Events Management Program 2023

This is an annual event that takes place once a year where different chefs from around the world gather to compete for the grand prize of "Best Event Planner Of The Year". It usually takes place at the Plaza Hotel in New York City.

Annual Big Eats Event 2024

The Annual Big Eats event is a fun event which takes place once a year where guests of all ages gather to plan a "Big Eats" party. It usually takes place at the Radisson Blu Hotel in New York City.



Weekly Trivia Nights Event, 2024 at Sofitel Hotel, London, UK

Weekly Trivia Nights Event 2024

The "Weekly Trivia Nights" event is a fun event which takes place on a weekly basis where customers are attracted to your restaurant space and being offered a friendly get together for the purposes of providing an entertaining experience. It usually takes place at the Sofitel Hotel in London, United Kingdom.

The "Annual Cocktail Making Nights" is a fun restaurant event which takes place on a yearly basis which showcases hands-on experience in cocktail making.
